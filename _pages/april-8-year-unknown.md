My Dear Mrs Haun
you see I think
of you at times
I want to see you
&amp; I expect that pleasure
if god is willing. Such
sad news from home
makes one feel rather
uncertain I hope you
&amp; your family are well
we are fine. I can
loosing friend to
seem my gold friends
next sumer I claim
Q. my home still
with love to all
E.W.

Mrs Mollie Haun
Quincy
Calif
Plumas Co.
