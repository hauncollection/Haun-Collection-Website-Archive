Chico Feb 2
I would [illegible]
do this in [illegible]
ter much snow
&amp; cold to all linger
to do so. I wish
you very well
of them I hope you
are well. My love
to you &amp; entire family
We are fine. Hays
[illegible] 1st
of Feb Fanie had truly
of cold hue, last
no snow you ad
know built of it
with love I am yours E.W.

Mrs John Haun
Quincy
California
Plumas Co.
